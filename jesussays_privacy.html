Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Jesus Says ("we," "our," or "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Conversation Data: Audio recordings and transcriptions of your conversations when using the "Talk to me, son" feature
  • Authentication Information: When you sign in with Apple, Google, or as a guest
  • Device Information: Device ID, app version, and platform information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: App performance and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-Generated Responses: Your conversations are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate Bible verse suggestions
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Authentication: Verify your identity when signing in
  • Analytics: Understand how the App is used (anonymized data)
  • Advertising: Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Your conversation transcripts are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate Bible verse suggestions
  • Data Shared: Conversation text, device context, and user locale
  • Privacy Policy: Google AI Privacy Policy
  • Note: Google processes your conversation data according to their privacy policy

4.2 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Backend services, authentication, and App Check
  • Data Shared: Device ID, app version, authentication tokens
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

4.3 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements in the App
  • Data Shared: Device information, advertising ID (if available)
  • Privacy Policy: AdMob Privacy Policy

4.4 Apple Sign In / Google Sign In

  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Data Shared: Authentication tokens, basic profile information (if you choose to share)
  • Privacy Policies:
    • Apple Privacy Policy
    • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Some data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Conversation history and user preferences may be stored securely in Firebase
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure

6. Data Retention

  • Conversation Data: Stored locally on your device and may be retained in Firebase for service functionality
  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained indefinitely

You have the right to:
