# Privacy Policy **Last Updated: February 27, 2026** ## 1. Introduction Jesus Says ("we," "our," or "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Information You Provide - **Conversation Data**: Your voice input is processed on-device and transcribed to text; only the transcribed text is transmitted to our backend for AI processing - **Authentication Information**: When you sign in with Apple or use the App with an anonymous session - **Device Information**: Device ID, app version, and platform information ### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information - **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App - **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers - **Log Data**: App performance and error logs - **Advertising Identifier (IDFA)**: If you grant tracking permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt, your device's Advertising Identifier may be collected by Google AdMob for personalized advertising ## 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: - **Provide AI-Generated Responses**: Your conversations are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate Bible verse suggestions - **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience - **Authentication**: Verify your identity when signing in - **Analytics**: Understand how the App is used (anonymized data) - **Advertising**: Display advertisements through Google AdMob. If you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission, ads may be personalized using your device's Advertising Identifier ## 4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS) When you first open the App, iOS will display an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking whether you allow the App to track your activity for advertising purposes. If you grant permission, your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) may be shared with Google AdMob to serve personalized ads. If you deny permission, only non-personalized ads will be shown. You can change this permission at any time in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**. ## 5. Third-Party Services ### 5.1 Google Gemini AI - **Purpose**: Your conversation transcripts are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate Bible verse suggestions - **Data Shared**: Conversation text, device context, and user locale - **Privacy Policy**: [Google AI Privacy Policy](https://ai.google.dev/terms) - **Note**: Google processes your conversation data according to their privacy policy ### 5.2 Firebase (Google) - **Purpose**: Backend services, authentication, and App Check security - **Data Shared**: Device ID, app version, authentication tokens - **Privacy Policy**: [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) ### 5.3 Google AdMob - **Purpose**: Display advertisements in the App - **Data Shared**: Device information and Advertising Identifier (IDFA), subject to your ATT choice - **Privacy Policy**: [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543) ### 5.4 Apple App Store (In-App Purchases) - **Purpose**: Processing of in-app purchases (premium ad-free access and Reflection packs of 20 or 100 Reflections) - **Data Shared**: Purchase transactions, product identifiers, and verification data are processed by Apple; we receive verification results to grant premium status and Reflection balance - **Privacy Policy**: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) ### 5.5 Apple Sign In - **Purpose**: Optional user authentication that links your account to your Apple ID, enabling purchase restoration and data persistence across reinstalls - **Data Shared**: Authentication tokens, basic profile information (if you choose to share with the App) - **Privacy Policy**: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) ## 6. Data Storage and Security - **Local Storage**: Conversation history and user preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage where applicable - **Cloud Storage**: Authentication tokens and App Check data are stored securely via Firebase. We do not store your conversation content in the cloud - **Security**: We use industry-standard security measures, including iOS Keychain for sensitive data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure ## 7. Data Retention - **Conversation Data**: Stored locally on your device; not retained on our servers - **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active - **Analytics Data**: Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained indefinitely ## 8.
