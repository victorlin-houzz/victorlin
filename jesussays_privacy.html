Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Jesus Says ("we," "our," or "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. You may use the App's core features without manually creating an account or signing in. If the App creates an anonymous session for service operation, or if you choose to sign in with Apple, we collect and use information as described below.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Conversation Data: Your voice input may be processed on-device or via Apple's Speech Recognition service, depending on your device and language settings, before being sent to our servers as text
  • Personal Reflection (Confession) Transcripts: Audio reflections you record in the Personal Reflection feature may be processed on-device or via Apple's Speech Recognition service, depending on your device and language settings, before being sent to our servers as text for spiritual guidance generation
  • Devotion Journey Transcripts: Daily audio check-ins you record in the Devotion Journey feature may be processed on-device or via Apple's Speech Recognition service, depending on your device and language settings, before being sent to our servers as text for AI-personalized devotional responses
  • Authentication Information: When you choose to sign in with Apple, or when the App uses an anonymous session for service operation. Core features do not require manual registration
  • Device Information: Device ID, app version, and platform information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: App performance and error logs
  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA): If you grant tracking permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt, your device's Advertising Identifier may be collected by Google AdMob for personalized advertising

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-Generated Responses: Your conversations are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate Bible verse suggestions
  • In-App Bible Reading: When you tap a verse reference, the App displays the full passage from Bible text included in the app; no additional personal data is sent for this feature
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Authentication: Verify your identity when you choose to sign in with Apple or when the App maintains an anonymous session
  • Analytics: Understand how the App is used (anonymized data)
  • Advertising: Display advertisements through Google AdMob. If you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission, ads may be personalized using your device's Advertising Identifier

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

After you review and accept our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy on first launch, iOS may display an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking whether you allow the App to track your activity for advertising purposes. If you grant permission, your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) may be shared with Google AdMob to serve personalized ads. If you deny permission, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Your conversation transcripts are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate Bible verse suggestions
  • Data Shared: Conversation text, device context, and user locale
  • Privacy Policy: Google AI Privacy Policy
  • Note: Google processes your conversation data according to their privacy policy

5.2 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Backend services, authentication, and App Check security
  • Data Shared: Device ID, app version, authentication tokens
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

5.3 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements in the App
  • Data Shared: Device information and Advertising Identifier (IDFA), subject to your ATT choice
  • Privacy Policy: AdMob Privacy Policy

5.4 Apple App Store (In-App Purchases)

  • Purpose: Processing of in-app purchases (Premium Lifetime ad-free access and Reflection packs of 5, 20, 50, or 100 Reflections)
  • Data Shared: Purchase transactions, product identifiers, and verification data are processed by Apple; we receive verification results to grant premium status and Reflection balance
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

5.5 Apple Sign In

  • Purpose: Optional user authentication that links your account to your Apple ID, enabling purchase restoration and data persistence across reinstalls
  • Data Shared: Authentication tokens, basic profile information (if you choose to share with the App)
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

5.6 Error Reporting

  • Purpose: Capture application errors for debugging and stability improvements
  • Data Shared: In the current release, the App logs errors only in local debug output and does not send crash data to a third-party crash analytics provider
  • Future Changes: If off-device crash reporting is enabled in a future release, this Privacy Policy and any applicable platform disclosures will be updated before rollout

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Conversation history and user preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage where applicable. Bible text used for in-app verse display is loaded from resources included in the app and is not transmitted to or from our servers for that feature
  • Cloud Storage: Authentication tokens and App Check data are stored securely via Firebase. We do not store your conversation content on our servers; however, your conversation text is transmitted to Google's Gemini AI for processing — Google retains this data according to their own privacy policy (see §5.1)
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures, including iOS Keychain for sensitive data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure

7. Data Retention

  • Conversation, Reflection, and Devotion Transcripts: Stored locally on your device; not retained on our servers after the AI response is generated
  • Account Data: Retained in Firebase (Firestore collections users/, devotion/, and purchases/) as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time via the Delete Account option in Settings or by emailing jesussays889@gmail.com
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained indefinitely
